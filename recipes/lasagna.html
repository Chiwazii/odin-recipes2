Description

Layers of pure comfort, lasagna is the kind of dish that wraps you in a warm, cheesy hug with every bite.

Tender pasta sheets cradle rich, savory meat sauce, while creamy ricotta and gooey mozzarella melt together in perfect harmony.

A sprinkle of Parmesan adds that salty, golden finish — the kind that makes you sneak just one more bite.

Every forkful is a little bit of everything — saucy, cheesy, and oh-so-satisfying.

Whether it's a cozy family dinner or leftovers straight from the fridge at midnight, lasagna never misses.
